# Healthy and sane 

## Physical
* Exercise 
* Eat properly - key: SMALL DINNERS
* Drink water
* Schedule doctor appointment
* Schedule dental appointment
* Take medicine / fiber
* Sleep
* Do hobby:
  * 1 that makes money
  * 1 that keeps you fit
  * 1 that is creative

## Mental
* Read
* Meditate
* Chat with someone you like
* Stay off mass social media
